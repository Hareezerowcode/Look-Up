Retailer Privacy Policy

Lookup ("we", "us", "our") understands the importance of data protection for retailers ("you", "your", also referred to as "registered users" in the End User Licence Agreement (EULA)

Lookup is a technology company built for the retail industry. We recognise that the information retailers provide to us can be commercially sensitive to their business, therefore we treat this information with great care. This Retailer Privacy Policy ("Policy") is designed to clearly outline what information we receive from you, how we use it, for what purpose and the legal bases for that use. We have made every effort to draft this document so that it is transparent and easily understandable.

This Policy applies to your use of the Services (as defined in the EULA) unless specified otherwise. Please also read the EULA which describes the terms under which you use our Services. Your interactions with the public section of our Website is governed by the Lookup Privacy Policy

Lookup is the data controller responsible for your information when you use our Services and our contact details are set out below.

This document is organised as follows:

  • Section A of this document explains how we process personal information.
  • Section B explains how we process non-personal information, such as product information.
  • Section C explains some circumstances in which your information may be shared to third parties. The circumstances explained in Section C apply to both personal and non-personal information. Additional circumstances that apply only to personal information, or only to non-personal information, are explained in their respective sections.
  • Section D includes a list of “Definitions” to help you understand a number of terms referenced throughout the Policy.
  • Section E explains how we will notify you of changes to this policy and how contact us if you have questions.

Section B - Product Information

To enable the Services to operate, we require every store on Lookup to send us product information. We use multiple methods to collect product information:

  • We may supply your store with a hardware device (the "Lookup box") which connects to your barcode scanner. We may also supply your store with a Lookup box to connect to your receipt printer. Whenever the scanner is used, the Lookup box sends us the barcode information. And whenever the receipt printer is used the Lookup box connected to it sends us receipt information.
  • The Lookup box can only transmit barcode information from the barcode scanner it is connected to, or receipt information from the receipt printer it is connected to. The Lookup box does not download any information from your POS, nor does it integrate with any of the software running on your POS. Any information stored on the POS cannot be read by the lookup box and will never be transmitted to us by the Lookup box.
  • You may also send us product information by way of manual upload.

How we use product information

We will use this information to:

  • Provide you with the Services and any other services that you request from us, for example to display and promote your products online, increasing online visibility of your store for the purpose of creating more in-store visits.
  • ensure a good consumer experience by displaying accurate product information online. For example, we display the stock status of products so that consumers have added information about the products you sell, increasing the likelihood of an in-store visit.

Additional product information supplied by retailers

  • We will use any additional product information supplied by you (e.g. manual upload information) to improve the Services as a whole. The additional product information will be combined and compared with our other sources of product information to ensure that we present the best and most accurate product information to consumers.
  • For example, should the additional basic product details you supply be sufficient to display a product on Lookup for which we previously had incomplete information, both you and any other retailer who sells that product will benefit by having the product appear on your respective Lookup pages.

Who we may share the information with

  • Your product information is commercially sensitive to your business. With the exceptions of the limited and defined scenarios listed in this section we do not share the information you provide to us without your express consent. We do not share your Lookup page usage information to any third parties.
  • We do not share your product information with your competitors, or third parties to advertise their services to you personally or to your business.
  • We may share your product information to promote your store, your products, or help customers find your store. To achieve this goal we may share your product information with search engines, local listing services and advertising platforms. For example, we may send product information to search engines such as Google to promote the visibility of your store or products. A list of external providers we currently work with on your behalf is available here.
  • We may share product information to create market level reports in which no individual store or retailer is identified. Any third party service provider involved in the creation of reports must agree to only use the information we provide to them for the defined purpose that it was sent.
  • We may share anonymised product information with third parties e.g. aggregated or statistical information about the popularity of products in different geographic regions based on an anonymous sample of retailers.

Please see also Section C for some additional circumstances in which information may be shared with third parties.

Section D - Definitions

For the purposes of this policy the following terms are defined:

  • "Anonymised information" means information in which no individual retailer or store is identified.
  • "Barcode information" means the barcode number of a product (also known as European Article Number "EAN", Universal Product Code "UPC", Global Trade Item Number "GTIN"), the approximate time of scanning and the serial number of the shopgro box or other account identifier.
  • "Basic product details” means the product name, image, description and other product details e.g. ingredients and nutritional information in food products.
  • "Basic store details" means the information you provide to us about your store when you sign-up for our Service or at later date, e.g. store name, store address, store contact information and opening hours. You may also provide details of your store type or category, approximate store size and the number of POS systems in your store.
  • "Integration information" means information sent to us using a POS integration.
  • "Inventory information” means information about product quantities in a store location, as supplied by you or estimated from historical barcode information, receipt information, integration information or basic product details.
  • "Manual upload information" means any product information that you manually send to us, for example by sending us a spreadsheet or CSV file.
  • "POS” means Point of Sale system.
  • "POS integration” means when our Services are directly integrated with your POS. When Lookup is integrated in this way you authorize your POS provider to transmit information to us, for example as defined by the Application Programming Interface (API) or other permissions defined by the POS software as determined by you.
  • "Lookup Page" means the unique webpage created by us for every store on shopgro and forms part of the Services
  • "Lookup page usage information" means usage information relating to your Lookup page, for example number of visits to your Lookup page or other similar metrics displayed on the private section of your page.
  • "Product information” means any combination of basic product details, barcode information, receipt information, inventory information, integration information and basic store details.
  • "Receipt information" means information related to a particular sales transaction or transaction receipt, including product price and quantity, as obtained from the printed receipt or POS integration.
